### Modeling a Plate

Add a circle, increase vertices to 100, radius to 0.12m, 
Make sure to bring 3D cursor on plane. circle will appear on 3D cusror.

<img width="660" alt="{7FE36402-ACF2-44C6-B05B-B2BA1ECC1496}" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6303a70a-9f98-4b3d-b160-a5b7403c31f0">

You can switch between select mode vertices : 1, edges : 2, faces : 3.

<img width="576" alt="{9F6987A2-BF71-4B8D-B208-66652206D9A0}" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ccc08daa-870e-432f-ab39-41aafdafe703">

Select all vertices of circle and press `E` key on keyboard, and move cursor up, increase the length little bit, then scale by `S` key outerward.

Then change to Edit Mode, select edges, and press `CTRL + B` for bevels and stop overlap using `C` key. Then make the plate round.

### Shrinking the camera size

select camera, from viewport or in collections, Then a camera icon will appear select it, then go to viewport display , Adjust size of camera according to your need.

<img width="828" alt="{00B08E7F-69CE-4E87-A6B0-C53B6CA78088}" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3e25cab7-d373-43fe-ba91-dffb0e6693af">

### Framing the camera

Adjust camera by clicking use fly navigation by `Shift + ~`, or `G` move camera by cursor, adjust sens by scroll up and down, use `Q` or `E` for going down and up, W for forward and `S` for backward and left for `A` and `D` for right, to confirm changes press left mouse button.
